Management
This grass increases on areas grazed during the
winter and on which grazing is deferred during the
summer. Burning does not easily damage Florida
dropseed.

Establishment
Growth starts in the late spring. It produces
seedheads in October. It usually scattered throughout
plant community, although pure stands do occur. It is
adapted to poorly drained soils that have a low pH.
